I am a Data Scientist with a master's degree in Big Data Science from Queen Mary University of London. I enjoy solving challenging problems using big data technologies, machine learning, and data analytics.
Even though I did my master's in Big Data Science, I come from a Mechanical Engineering background! During my master's, I had to spend more hours on CS topics that my peers were already familiar with. Strangly enough, this made it all more exciting to me! I saw an opportunity to learn about interesting technologies that shape our modern world and challenge myself along the way. So I took this opportunity and worked hard to earn my master's degree with first class honors [92/100] and build problem-solving skills and implement them in various projects! I am passionate about science, technology and art. If I'm not coding or debugging, I'm probably painting, reading a scifi novel, watching a movie, or spending time with my dog.
